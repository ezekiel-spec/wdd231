Bwari Trails Guide - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Bwari Trails Guide

Reason: This name highlights our focus on guiding locals and visitors through the scenic hiking and biking trails in Bwari, Abuja.

Site Purpose

This site will serve as a comprehensive guide for outdoor enthusiasts in Bwari. It will include detailed trail information, local wildlife insights, and trail condition updates.

Scenarios

  • Which trails in Bwari are safe for families with children?
  • Are there any guided tours or trail groups I can join?

Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#228B22): Used for headings and highlights.
  • Ivory (#FFFFF0): Used as the background color for a clean and nature-inspired feel.

Typography

  • Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif
  • Usage: Headings and body content throughout the site.
